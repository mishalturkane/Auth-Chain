How AuthChain works

  1. Upload: Employer or university uploads certificate PDF/image.
  2. Extract: OCR extracts fields; AI analyzes visuals for tampering.
  3. Verify: Hash/QR checks + registry cross-match (or blockchain anchor check).
  4. Report: System returns Valid / Suspicious / Forged with evidence package.

Example evidence & metrics

Duplicate / Cloned Certificates

Same certificate reused across multiple identities. 5–10 detections / 1,000 checks.

Tampered marksheets

Grades altered to meet cutoffs — common tactic in fraud rings.

Forged seals / signatures

Visually convincing forgeries often found in major sting operations.
